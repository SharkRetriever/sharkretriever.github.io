Excerpts from The Handbook of Cubik Math (Chapter 4)

Dec 19, 2017

Put simply, The Handbook of Cubik Math is an outdated reference on solving the Rubik’s cube, where the text seems to be read in through a 1980s OCR device, and left unedited afterwards.

The notation is old ( anyone?), and of course, some of the content seems outright irrelevant, such as the chapter on antislice moves. But then again, it could just be the fact that I’m not into studying antislice moves.

For these reasons, I have copied the parts of the book that I found interesting to serve as a reference for other cubing nerds out there. Note that the wording has been changed in multiple places to suit more modern readers.
