Site Recommendations

These are the ones I’ve got so far.

  • 4D Euclidean Space dissects 4D shapes layer by layer so you can see how they’re all constructed.
  • Acoustic Phonetics – especially interesting is the “Formants” section, relating spectrograms to the IPA vowel trapezium.
  • Arch Chinese is amazing if you know how to speak Mandarin, but need to get better at reading and writing it.
  • MagicCube4D provides some nice 4D twisty puzzles for whenever 3D twisty puzzles get too boring.
  • MagicCube5D provides some nice 5D n^5 twisty puzzles for whenever 4D twisty puzzles get too boring (seriously?)
  • Tabular Method for Integration by Parts – A concise way to integrate by parts, though it helps to know the long way first.
  • Visualizing Hyperbolic Honeycombs goes over all possible hyperbolic honeycombs with Schläfli symbol {p,q,r}.
