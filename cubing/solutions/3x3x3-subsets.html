Clockwork Cube 3x3x3 (CC3)

For this case, instead of doing just 1-layer turns, each turn made has to have a corresponding two-layer turn done after. For example, U on a CC3 is actually u U. Of course, rotations remain the same as on a normal 3x3x3.

This puzzle closely resembles the gear cube, but presents different edge orientation cases.
